Holiday Packages Policy

Home > Packages Policy

1. Package Inclusions

Our holiday packages typically include accommodation, specified meals (breakfast/dinner), airport transfers, and guided sightseeing as mentioned in the itinerary. Flight tickets may or may not be included based on the package type selected.

2. Package Exclusions

Common exclusions involve personal expenses (laundry, tips), optional entry fees for monuments, travel insurance, visa fees for international trips, and taxes unless specified.

3. Changes to Package

Any changes requested after booking confirmation are subject to availability and may incur additional charges from the airline/hotel partners.

4. Force Majeure

Udaan Airways is not responsible for any delay or cancellation of tours due to natural disasters, political unrest, or unexpected health emergencies (Force Majeure events). In such cases, we will try to reschedule the trip without additional costs wherever possible.

5. Insurance Recommendation

We highly recommend all travelers to purchase comprehensive travel insurance to cover unexpected cancellations, medical emergencies, or baggage loss during the trip.
