Privacy Policy

Home > Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and payment details when you book a flight or package with us. This information is necessary to process your bookings and provide customer support.

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Process and confirm your travel bookings
  • Provide 24x7 customer support and assistance
  • Send you updates, promotions, and marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services based on user feedback and behavior

3. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. We use industry-standard SSL encryption for all data transfers and maintain secure storage systems to protect your personal information from unauthorized access.

4. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us understand how you use our site, save your preferences, and provide personalized content. You can manage your cookie settings through your browser.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external websites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information held by us. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact our HR department.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at hr@udaanairways.com.
